A compact combat arena where an adaptive opponent approaches, retreats, and pressures the player based on health, distance, and momentum.

Combat Notes

Move with arrow keys or WASD. Press space to attack when close.

How it works

The enemy approaches when far away.
The enemy retreats when badly hurt.
If you are weak and close, it pressures hard.
Momentum rises when your attacks land.
Cooldowns keep combat readable and fair.
