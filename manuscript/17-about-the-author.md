# About the Author — Laurence Lars Svekis

Laurence Lars Svekis is an instructor, developer, author, and course creator who has taught **over 2 million students worldwide** through online courses, books, newsletters, workshops, and technical tutorials.

He is known for creating practical, project-based learning experiences in areas such as:
- JavaScript and front-end development
- Google Apps Script and automation
- AI-assisted learning and development
- modern digital skills for creators and professionals

Laurence’s teaching style focuses on clarity, structure, and real-world application. He believes people learn best when they build things that work, understand why the code works, and then extend the result through curiosity and experimentation.

This book reflects that philosophy through the Vibe Learning and Vibe Coding systems: learn through building, explain through systems, and refine through intentional iteration.
